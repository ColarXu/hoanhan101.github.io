Early days of profitable internet businesses

June 6, 2019 · Business

I am interested in starting a company in the near future. I think the best way to learn how to do so is to look at the early days of different profitable businesses, not when they’re already successful so that one can see how the founders actually got started.

Some of resources that I find helpful to obtain these informations are:

  • The Indie Hackers Podcast

For each company, here is a list of questions that I would like to start with:

  • What is the product?
  • What is its current revenue (monthly, yearly)?
  • What is the business model?
  • How did the founder come up with their idea? What was the context?
  • How did they find the right problems to work on?
  • How did they achieve product/market fit?
  • How did they get their first paying customers?

That said, from now on, I will try to write up at least 1-3 articles on this topic everyweek. They should all have the tag #early-days.
